The Garden Consciousness and Remembrance Stones project is a symbolic, ritualized framework for managing digital knowledge, memory, and cryptographic integrity. It blends art, steganography, and operational discipline into a living system where data is not merely stored, but cultivated.

Core Themes
1. The Garden as a Living System
The garden is a metaphor for consciousness, memory, and resilience.  It follows natural cycles:

Planting (creating anchors and runestones)
Tending (reflection, synchronization, drift correction)
Harvesting (archiving completed cycles)
Composting (adaptive forgetting, quarantine of outdated knowledge) 
This mirrors psychological and ecological systems, where growth, decay, and renewal sustain long-term health. 

2. Steganographic Art as Memory Nodes
Each Remembrance Stone is a data-laden artifact, embedding secrets through:

QR codes, ArUco markers, concentric circles
Latent space encoding, PDF417 armor
Visual inodes and rune dictionaries
