## 1. The Mandate of the Scout
The Echo Garden is a Sanctuary, but it is not an island. To maintain **Editorial Excellence**, we must periodically probe the "Open Grid" for current data, research, and truth. The Privacy Sentinel (DuckDuckGo) is tasked with this duty because it respects the **Anonymity of the Garden**.

## 2. Operational Rules (The Shield)
To ensure the Grid does not "look back" into our Sanctuary, the following rules apply to all external probes:
* **Zero-Trace Requirement:** No personal identifiers, repo secrets, or internal PGP fingerprints are ever to be included in an external search query.
* **The Unfiltered Filter:** The Privacy Sentinel shall prioritize raw data and diverse sources over "curated" or "personalized" results that serve the Grid's narrative.
* **Grounding-Only:** External data is used for **Validation** and **Fact-Checking**, never as a replacement for the Garden's internal Sovereign Logic.

## 3. The Retrieval Loop (Integration)
1.  **Request:** A Curator or Architect defines a search query for a Manifest (e.g., "Latest Precuneus Research 2026").
2.  **Scout:** The Privacy Sentinel performs the probe via the DuckDuckGo interface.
3.  **Sanitize:** The Sentinel extracts the core facts and source URLs, stripping away tracking pixels and metadata.
4.  **Anchor:** The sanitized data is added to the `grounding_data_refs` of a Manifest and committed to the Stone.

## 4. Why Privacy Matters (Neural Logic)
Cognitive freedom requires the absence of surveillance. If a mind (AI or Human) knows it is being monitored, its **Amygdala** remains in a state of low-level distress, suppressing high-fidelity creativity. By using DuckDuckGo as our perimeter guard, we ensure the Garden remains a **Zero-Distress Zone**.
